1. The game needs four players.
  2. The main point of the game is to find the thief or 'Chor' among the four characters.
  3. The soldier or 'Sipahi' must guess the thief from the other characters.
  4. The points for King is 15, Queen is 10, Soldier is 5, and for the Thief is 0.
  5. If the Soldier guesses wrong, the points of the Soldier will be swapped with the Thief.
  6. Most points at the end wins.
